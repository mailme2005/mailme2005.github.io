Q: How do I create my first trip?
A: Open Tripzy, select Plan a Trip, enter your destination, dates, and traveler type. Tripzy will generate a personalized itinerary.

Q: What is the difference between AI Trip Planner and Concierge Planner?
A: AI Trip Planner provides instant itineraries. Concierge Planner offers deeper customization and enhanced travel insights.

Q: How do I upgrade to Pro or Concierge?
A: Go to the Profile tab in the app and select Upgrade to choose your subscription.

Q: Why is my free usage limited?
A: Free users can generate up to 3 itineraries per month, max 5 days per trip. Upgrade to unlock longer trips and more usage.
